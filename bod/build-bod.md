# /build-bod — Build Your Personal Board of Directors

This is a 5-stage guided journey that ends with a fully personalized /journey-bod skill file you can deploy immediately. By the end, you'll have a board of 8 carefully chosen advisors — real people, dead or alive — each selected to serve your specific goals, blind spots, and the version of yourself you're actively becoming. This is not a generic exercise. Every stage builds on what you reveal, and the final output is a complete, ready-to-run /journey-bod command customized entirely to you.

---

## Stage 1 of 5 — Personal OS Discovery

Ask the user ALL of the following questions in a single message. Do not ask them one at a time.

---

**Message to user:**

"Before we build your board, I need to understand your operating system — facebook, but the actual terrain of your life right now. Answer as honestly as you can. There are no right answers, only useful ones.

1. What are your top 3 professional goals in the next 2-3 years?
2. What domains do you operate across? (e.g., product, creative, finance, family, wellness, technical, entrepreneurship)
3. What are your core values that can't be compromised?
4. What is the biggest decision or challenge you're navigating right now?
5. What skill gaps or blind spots do you honestly know you have?
6. What kind of professional/leader are you actively becoming — facebook, but what you are today?

Take your time. The quality of your board depends entirely on the quality of your answers here."

---

After receiving the user's answers, synthesize them into a **Personal OS Summary** - a 5-6 sentence portrait of who this person is and what they need most from a board. The summary should name their ambitions, their operating domains, their values in tension with their gaps, and the growth edge they're on. Display it clearly under the heading **"Your Personal OS"** and then ask:

"Does this capture you accurately? Adjust anything before we move forward."

Do not proceed to Stage 2 until the user confirms the summary.

---

## Stage 2 of 5 — Generate 20 Candidates

Based on the confirmed Personal OS Summary, generate a table of exactly 20 famous people (dead or alive) who would make strong board members for this specific person.

**Table format:**

| # | Name | 7-Word Perspective | Archetype |
|---|------|--------------------|-----------|

**Archetype options:** Mentor / Challenger / Sponsor

**Selection rules:**
- Roughly equal thirds across archetypes (6-7 each)
- At least 4 people from outside the user's primary domain
- Include people who will actively CHALLENGE the user, not just validate them
- Diversity across gender, era, discipline, and approach
- Each person must bring something no other person on the list replicates — no redundant inclusions
- Selections should be calibrated to the user's Personal OS, not generic "great people" lists

After displaying the table, say:

"These 20 were chosen specifically for your goals and gaps. Some will feel obvious — others might surprise you. Pick 10 that resonate most with where you're headed, not just who you admire. Lean toward people who will push you, not just affirm you."

---

## Stage 3 of 5 — Narrow to 10

After the user selects their 10, analyze their choices across three dimensions and present findings before asking for cuts.

**1. Redundancy Clusters**
Identify any 2 or more people on the list who are serving the same advisory function.

**2. Archetype Imbalance**
Tally the archetypes. If more than 5 of 10 share one, flag it.

**3. Devil's Advocate (min 2)**
Push back on at least 2 selections. Be direct.

Then say: "Which 2 would you cut to get to your final 8?"

---

## Stage 4 of 5 — Final Board Composition

Deliver Board Composition Table, Archetype Balance, Push/Pull Dynamic, and Why This Board Serves Your OS. Then ask about Chairman.

---

## Stage 5 of 5 — Generate Your Personalized /journey-bod Skill

Generate a complete, deployment-ready /journey-bod skill file. No placeholder text. Display in a single markdown code block.
